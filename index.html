About Me

A Boise native, Mark attended school locally and earned a Bachelors Degree in Buisness from Brown Mackie College

Starting with several self-taught courses on programming, learning the basics of HTML/CSS, the fundementals of javascript, and a bit of Python, he eventually found Boise Codeworks. Once completing the course Mark was approached by the instructor and CEO and because of the unique balance of his personality and coding skill has taken on the new challenge of teaching others coding.

When he is not instructing, Mark likes to spend time with his family, playing and building VR games, Leading D&D campaigns as dungeon master, and Photography.
